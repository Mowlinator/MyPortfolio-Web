Get to know me!

Hey! It's Addison Mowl and I'm a Gallery Support located in Detroit. In my current role, I manage inventory, organize the movement of furniture, and provide exceptional client services support. I am responsible for ticketing merchandise in preparation for display, and for creating a luxury service experience in every interaction. I also coordinate and execute merchandise floor sets while upholding the visual integrity of the Gallery. I am able to read and audit planograms and visual maps, and offer a creative point of view on company directives. I continuously drive results through team engagement, empowerment, and accountability.

I enjoy reading and learning about new topics, and have a curious and inquisitive mind. I believe that my hobbies and interests allow me to bring a unique perspective and creative energy to my work.

I have a wide range of skills and expertise, including proficiency in Blender and other 3D modeling software. I also have experience in coding and computer hardware, and possess excellent communication and troubleshooting skills. My exceptional analytical and problem-solving abilities allow me to deliver top results with a positive attitude no matter what the obstacle. I am committed to quality with exceptional attention to detail, and I have superior organization and time-management skills. Feel free to contact me here.

Projects Discover my portfolio of innovative and impactful projects that showcase my expertise in Design. Each project highlights my unique problem-solving approach, attention to detail, and commitment to delivering exceptional results.

My Minimalist Tiny Home

I used Blender to create this 3D render of my contemporary tiny home. The purpose of this project was to showcase my skills in creating functional and visually appealing architectural designs. The tiny home features clean lines, a large window, and a simple color palette, all of which are hallmarks of contemporary design. One of the challenges was making sure that the space felt open and airy despite its small size, but I was able to overcome this by using strategic furniture placement and natural lighting. The final result was a stunning and functional tiny home that would be perfect for those looking to downsize without sacrificing style.
